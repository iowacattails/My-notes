MISSION STATEMENT

Educating the People on what our Rights are, where they come from and how to effectively claim, exercise and defend them following due process of law.

Due Process of Law is the process that We the People have, as a matter of Right, to limit “government” per the Constitutions. This process is due to you in all situations involving the Law and the enforcement thereof.
